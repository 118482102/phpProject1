Sports

Football

I started playing football when i was 6 with my local team in Ardfert.
When i was 14 I was selected to play for the Kerry development squads and over 3 years with them I won two Munster medals
Nowadays i play with the Ardfert senior team.
I primarily play wing back but have played all over the field.

Hurling

I started playing hurling when i was 10 with the local team St.Brendans.
Nowadays i play with the senior team where i play wing forward.
Although far better at football in ardfert most are far more passionate about their hurling.

Cross Country
